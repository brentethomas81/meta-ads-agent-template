# Meta Platform — what changed (current as of 2026-06)

> Platform mechanics shift constantly. This file tracks the *current* rules so the agent doesn't give 2022 advice. Refresh when Meta ships major changes; date every entry.

## Targeting (the big one)
- **Detailed/interest targeting is deprecated.** Interest audiences built before Oct 2025 stopped delivering; detailed targeting was retired **Jan 15, 2026**. [verified]
- **Default to broad + Advantage+ Audience.** Hand-picking interests is no longer the play — feed Meta a conversion signal and good creative, let it find buyers. [verified]
- Custom Audiences and Lookalikes still work and are valuable as *seeds/signals* (your brand's 1% LAL off subscribers is exactly right).

## Campaign setup
- **CBO is standard** and usually outperforms manual ABO budgeting. [verified]
- **Advantage+ auto-activates** with: broad targeting + optimized (Advantage+) placements + campaign-level budget + a purchase/conversion optimization event. [verified]
- **Budget for learning:** aim for ~50× target CPA per week; below that, learning is slow and calls stay low-confidence. [verified]
- **Learning phase:** ~50 conversions/week to exit; first 3–7 days, don't edit. [verified]

## Creative formats Meta is pushing
- **Reels (9:16) first**, then 4:5 in feed (4:5 ~15% better than square in feed). [verified]
- Meta's own guidance leans into **creative diversification** + **strong 3-second hooks** + **sound on** (music/voiceover). [verified]
- UGC-style is the dominant high-performer. [verified]

## Implications for our brands
- **your brand:** run broad/Advantage+ Audience with the 1% LAL as a signal seed; optimize for the **Subscribe/Purchase** event (via Stripe CAPI), not traffic; lead with a 3-sec hook in the 9:16 reel; refresh creative every 1–2 weeks.
- Avoid building new interest stacks — they won't deliver.
