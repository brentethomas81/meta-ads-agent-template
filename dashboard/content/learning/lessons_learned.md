# Lessons Learned

Distilled, transferable lessons from real outcomes — per brand. These override the general Knowledge Vault benchmarks once a brand has enough data. The agent reads this every analysis and appends new lessons as patterns prove out.

## How a lesson earns its place
A pattern becomes a lesson when it (a) repeats across 2+ campaigns or ≥20 closed conversions, and (b) held up — i.e., acting on it actually moved CAC the predicted way. Speculation stays in the Decision Log until proven.
